## Direction for Havalgame3

Start as a new game, not from `havalgame2`.

Recommended concept:

- Haval pickup / tank-like vehicle in a low-poly night arena.
- Enemies: UFO drones.
- Controls: WASD/arrow movement, pointer or auto-aim shooting, mobile joystick later.
- Camera: third-person chase camera.
- Combat loop: dodge enemy shots, shoot UFOs, collect points/powerups.
- First milestone: one arena, one player vehicle, one UFO enemy type, bullets, collisions, score.
